# Echoscape Project Plan

## Brief Overview
Echoscape is an interactive map of Iran and Pakistan that presents sounds, music, soundscapes, and other audio content originating from that place, including audio generated by AI. The project invites users to explore how a place can be heard, interpreted, and represented through both human and machine-generated sound.

## Main Goal
The main goal of Echoscape is to make the biases and assumptions of AI visible. By comparing human-made and AI-generated audio, the project encourages users to question how technology can shape cultural perception, reinforce stereotypes, or simplify complex local identities.

## Target Audience
- Students from multicultural backgrounds in different countries
- Visitors of media art exhibitions
- People interested in sound, interactive media, digital culture, and critical AI

## Project Objectives
1. Create an engaging and visually clear interactive map.
2. Curate audio content that reflects the region’s sonic identity.
3. Show how AI-generated audio can differ from human-created sound.
4. Encourage reflection on cultural representation, memory, and technology.
5. Make the experience accessible, immersive, and meaningful for diverse audiences.

## Core Features
- Interactive map interface for exploring a specific region
- Audio layers for sounds, music, soundscapes, and AI-generated content
- Visual design that supports storytelling and discovery
- Clear presentation of differences between human and AI-generated audio
- A reflective experience that invites discussion about bias and representation

## Tools
- VS Code for development and project organization
- GitHub for version control and collaboration
- Canva for visual assets and presentation design
- ChatGPT for content generation, ideation, and writing support
- GitHub Copilot for coding assistance
- P5js for interactive and generative media development

## Suggested Workflow
1. Research the selected region and gather audio material.
2. Define the visual and interactive structure of the map.
3. Prototype the interface and audio interactions.
4. Develop the project in P5js and related tools.
5. Test the experience with users and refine the concept.
6. Prepare the final presentation for exhibitions or classroom use.

## Success Criteria
- The project clearly communicates how AI can influence sound representation.
- The interactive experience is engaging and easy to explore.
- The work invites reflection on cultural assumptions, identity, and technology.
